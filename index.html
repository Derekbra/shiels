PROCESS
Make My Shape creates a unique poster in 4 simple steps. When you start you will be asked to enter up to to 6 dates. The app will then create a series of shapes based on your dates. In step 2 you can add colour to your shapes. When you have done that you can edit your shape's size, angle and position. Once you are happy with the overall look you can add a personal note. Thats it, a simple process.
You can buy the poster in either A1, A2, A3 or A4 size and on a number of paper finishes. After making your choices youpay for it and enter your address and delivery details. The poster gets delivered to your door free of charge in a hard tube with end caps keeping your poster pristine clean.

DELIVERY Every Make My Shape poster is delivered to your door and is included in your final price. The whole process takes about 2 weeks. This is from your order to delivery to your front door. When you create your poster and pay for it it gets sent to our printer who produces the product, inserts it into a tube and posts it. It then gets collected by the postal service who deliver it to you. Depending where you are in the world it could take up to 2 weeks for delivery but no more. If you have any issues contact us with your order number and we will try our best to help you out.

PRODUCT Each poster is printed on high quality acid free stock. Printed onto 170gsm paper stock which is sturdy and heavy. We offer 3 finishes for your poster, matt, silk and gloss. Matt is non shiny and rough to the feel, silk is a mixture between matt and gloss. And gloss is shiny and hard to the touch.

Our printer is the best in the buisness and uses only the best machines to produce each poster. This ensures each print is high quality and the colour is vibrant and the print is sharp.

The final poster is inserted into a 2mm thick tube with end caps and posted to you. The poster will arrive to you in pristine condition, with no marks, creases or dogged corners.
